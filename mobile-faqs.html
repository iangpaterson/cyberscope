Frequently Asked Questions

How do I engage in interactive, real time, Q&A sessions with large numbers of viewers?

Just deliver your broadcast and encourage viewers to ask you questions that you can immediately respond to in your live stream.
You will only get comments and questions on the screen from users that view the stream within the CyberscopeTV app.
It is not possible for people who view your broadcasts on other social media to make comments.
It is possible for the public to view your streams on any IOS, Android, Tablet or PC, but broadcasts can only be delivered from an Android phone.
(IOS version in progress)

How do I delete my broadcasts?

Push the three line BAR at the top left of the screen and go to broadcasts.
You will see a list of all your streams and a red delete icon.

You will also see...
Followers, Following, Chat, Appointments and Logout.

How do I broadcast and share my CyberscopeTV LIVE video stream on social media?

After pushing the Red camera icon you will see the broadcast screen.
Enter the title and push the icon in the middle of the screen for LinkedIn, Facebook or Twitter.
Push the Start Streaming bar.

You are now streaming LIVE to all your social media, fans, friends and connections.

How do I contact a broadcaster?

Push the broadcasters icon picture at the bottom left of the video thumbnail.
You can Instant Message chat, Voice call, Video chat, or go to the members social media profile.
There is an appointment calendar if the broadcaster is offline.

How do I get paid for coaching?

In your profile add your fee rate for video chat.
This can be free if you would like to offer pro bono support.
Any member can engage you for the agreed rate and pay using the in app payment gateway.

What is the cost to join the CyberscopeTV coaching team?

It is free to join and advertise your services.
CyberscopeTV deduct a 30% transaction fee on all confidential one on one video chat calls.
This includes all online banking charges.

The first 5 minutes (introductory) video chat with a new broadcaster is free.
This enables you to get to know the person you will be working with.

I am looking for a Subject Matter Expert, Health Patient or Coach. How do I search for the right one?

Go to the search bar (magnifying glass icon) at the top of the screen and type in the keyword that is most relevant.
A list of broadcasts will appear that contain the word or phrase that matches your search.

View archived videos from that broadcaster and visit their social media profile to learn more about their qualifications and experience.

I am only looking for broadcasters where I live. How do I find them?

Go to the GPS map and push on the live or saved livestream icons in your chosen location.

About Cyberscope TV

Cyberscope TV is a crowd sharing platform for opportunites, challenges and technologies changing the world of Cyber Security.

We have secured our on screen comments and chat using an RSA algorithm. Video and audio chats use WebRTC technology which in its turn makes use of DTLS protocol by default. Datagram Transport Layer Security (DTLS) is used to provide communications privacy for datagram protocols. This fosters a secure signaling channel that cannot be tampered with. In other words, no eavesdropping or message forgery can occur on a DTLS encrypted connection.

As regards our storage and streaming, we use OAuth plus our additional authenticated and encrypted connection channels to deliver data only to authorised users.
