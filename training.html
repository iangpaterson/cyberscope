Data protection/EU GDPR Compliance

The EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) will supersede the Data Protection Act 1998 (DPA) on 25 May 2018. It introduces a number of key changes for all organisations that process personal information, including new rules on international data transfers, documenting data processing activities, performing data protection impact assessments and appointing data protection officers.

About Cyberscope TV

Cyberscope TV is a crowd sharing platform for opportunites, challenges and technologies changing the world of Cyber Security.

We have secured our on screen comments and chat using an RSA algorithm. Video and audio chats use WebRTC technology which in its turn makes use of DTLS protocol by default. Datagram Transport Layer Security (DTLS) is used to provide communications privacy for datagram protocols. This fosters a secure signaling channel that cannot be tampered with. In other words, no eavesdropping or message forgery can occur on a DTLS encrypted connection.

As regards our storage and streaming, we use OAuth plus our additional authenticated and encrypted connection channels to deliver data only to authorised users.
